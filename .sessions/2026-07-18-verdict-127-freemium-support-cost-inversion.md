## 💡 Session idea
The proof pins a DETERMINISTIC, KNOWN intent-decay law p(i)=P0·e^{−K·i} with K disclosed (K=0.001), so the contribution optimum n*=ln(V·P0/c)/K is a closed-form constant the operator can compute up front and stop at exactly. But in a real freemium funnel the decay rate K (and even P0, V, c) is UNKNOWN and must be ESTIMATED ONLINE from realized conversions as the cohort is acquired — so the operator faces a sequential optimal-stopping / exploration problem: acquire user i, observe convert/not, update K̂, and STOP at the estimated n̂*=ln(V·P0̂/ĉ)/K̂, which drifts as evidence accrues. The load-bearing generalization the current gate does NOT exercise is ESTIMATION RISK ON THE CAP: because n* = ln(·)/K is HYPERBOLIC in K, a small relative error in K̂ produces a LARGE absolute error in n̂* (∂n*/∂K = −ln(V·P0/c)/K² — at the pinned world a 10% under-estimate of K over-shoots the cap by ~180 users and can tip a positive program negative), and early-cohort conversions are a high-variance estimator of a small K, so a naive "stop when observed marginal contribution first goes negative" rule stops LATE (it waits for the realized loss) and systematically over-acquires. A genuinely different next VENTURE slice pins the online-cap-estimation mechanism: hold the declining-intent contribution frame but DRAW conversions sequentially under an unknown K, and pre-register (G-a) an estimation-risk gate — the terminal contribution under a plug-in stop-at-n̂*(K̂) rule falls significantly short of the oracle C(n*) by a measured regret matching the ∂n*/∂K sensitivity within 3σ (so "estimate the decay, not the optimum" is quantified); (G-b) a stopping-rule-necessity control — a conservative confidence-bounded stop (stop when the UPPER confidence bound on marginal contribution crosses zero) recovers ≥3σ more terminal contribution than the naive "stop at first realized negative" rule, proving the over-acquisition is the estimator lag, not the cap formula; and (G-c) a value-of-information gate — spending a fixed early-exploration budget to sharpen K̂ before committing the cap lifts terminal contribution by a ≥3σ margin over acquiring blind — converting this slice's static, oracle-known "cap at n*=ln(V·P0/c)/K" into the actionable, ≥3σ-gateable "K is unknown and n* is hyperbolically sensitive to it, so meter acquisition against a confidence-bounded estimate of the decay and pay to learn the yield curve before scaling" — a seed-pinnable online-estimation-of-the-contribution-optimum mechanism distinct from this slice's known-K closed-form optimum and from the outbox's static concavity anchor.
