The cure is to remove the second independent markup (two-part tariff at cost + fixed franchise fee, RPM / price ceiling, revenue-share, or vertical integration). The operator lesson sharpens from "kill the second markup" to "every added independent layer is a multiplicative surplus tax — collapse layers (buy-out, direct-sell, agency pricing) before the geometric decay strands the channel; a two-part tariff at ONE layer is a patch, but K layers need K cures or one integration."
