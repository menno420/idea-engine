## 💡 Session idea
"Compounding an iid multiplicative reward across a population makes wealth log-normal and concentrates it (Gini→1, median/mean→0 as the horizon grows) regardless of skill, while paying the SAME shocks additively on a fixed base stays fair" is a reusable design lens for any volume/horizon-scaled process that stacks a per-unit multiplicative reward on a running total. Whenever an economy is judged by its wealth spread and the reward compounds, the operator is missing that the inequality is manufactured by the multiplicative structure, not by any agent's edge — the same-shocks additive counterfactual is the load-bearing guard that isolates compounding as the cause. The transferable audit: if the goal is an egalitarian or bounded-spread economy, pay the reward on a FIXED base (additive), cap the compounding horizon, rebase/normalize periodically, or add a wealth-proportional sink; read a widening spread under compounding as the DESIGNED outcome of the reward, never as skill. Candidate future game/economy heads with the same multiplicative-process anchor and a freeze-the-compounding necessity control: an idle-game prestige multiplier whose stacking concentrates progress; a staking/interest yield whose spread is a compounding artifact; a multiplicative loot/XP multiplier whose leaderboard gap is manufactured — each a "compounding an iid multiplicative reward → log-normal concentration" claim with the same σ_T=σ·√horizon anchor and an additive same-shocks control that flattens it.
