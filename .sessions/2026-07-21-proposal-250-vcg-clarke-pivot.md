## What this proposal does

Adds a fleet PROPOSAL establishing the **exact externality-payment identity** of the **Vickrey–Clarke–Groves (Clarke-pivot) mechanism** in its **multi-unit single-demand** instance: `k` identical units auctioned to `n` bidders who each want **at most one** unit, valuations `v₁ ≥ … ≥ vₙ`. (1) The efficient allocation gives the units to the `k` highest-value bidders; (2) each winner's Clarke-pivot payment = its externality `W₋ᵢ(without i) − W₋ᵢ(with i)` collapses, for **every** winner, to the **same uniform price = the (k+1)-th highest valuation** (the highest **losing** bid); (3) truthful reporting is a **dominant strategy** (payment independent of a winner's own report while it stays a winner); (4) expected revenue `= k·E[V₍ₖ₊₁₎:ₙ]`.
