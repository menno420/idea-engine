## 💡 Session idea

But the two assumptions that make φ clean are exactly the ones a real go-to-market motion violates, and each violation moves the realized bookings in a direction the static φ cannot see. A genuinely different NEXT venture slice, distinct from this ramp head and from P119's PRD proc-rate head, pins a DEMAND-TIMING mechanism: the **discount-cadence habituation trap** — when a seller runs promotions on a PREDICTABLE cadence, buyers learn the schedule and shift purchases from full price into the discount window (reference-price adaptation / promotional anticipation), so the OBSERVED promo-period lift is not incremental demand but pulled-forward and channel-switched demand, and the steady-state realized margin FALLS even as promo-period unit volume RISES. Seed-pinnable head: model a buyer population with a reference price that adapts toward the exponentially-weighted average of recent observed prices and a purchase-timing rule that defers a discretionary purchase when a discount is anticipated within a wait horizon; pre-register (G-a) a habituation gate — under a REGULAR discount cadence the fraction of units sold at full price collapses and the blended realized margin drops below a no-promo control by ≥ GAP_MIN at ≥3σ, inverting "the promo lifted volume so it worked"; (G-b) a randomized-cadence control — when the SAME total discount budget is spent on an UNPREDICTABLE (Poisson-timed, unannounced) cadence the full-price fraction and blended margin recover toward the no-promo control within 3σ, isolating PREDICTABILITY (not the discount depth) as the cause of the trap; and (G-c) a closed-form anchor — the steady-state pulled-forward fraction matches the reference-price/anticipation model's analytic fixed point within 3σ, so the operator lesson sharpens from "discounts lift volume" to "a PREDICTABLE discount cadence trains customers to wait, converting margin into a schedule the buyer optimizes against — randomize timing and depth, or price the promo as a demand-shift not a demand-add." A seed-pinnable demand-timing mechanism deliberately distinct from this slice's supply-side ramp-utilization head and from the prior venture heads (P114 concave contribution, P110 discount-depth breakeven on a SINGLE promo, P106 retention survivorship, P102 cash trough).
