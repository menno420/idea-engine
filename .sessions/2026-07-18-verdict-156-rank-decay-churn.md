Each period a surviving player draws a fresh per-match enjoyment u ~ Uniform[0, U_MAX] and forms the loss-averse match value v = u + DELTA·(p − LAMBDA·(1−p)) (a prospective rank gain valued +DELTA, a loss valued −LAMBDA·DELTA); if v>0 the player PLAYS (win w.p. p: current += DELTA; else current −= DELTA), counts one activity, resets idle pain to 0; else idle: apply decay current −= d (raising future p — the folk recovery channel), accrue pain += LAMBDA·d, and QUIT permanently if pain > PATIENCE (churn).
