## What this proposal does

But a committee of K=16 drawn by **uniform sortition WITHOUT replacement** is UNSAFE iff it contains ≥ T = floor(K/3)+1 = 6 Byzantine members (a K-node BFT quorum tolerates floor((K−1)/3) = 5).
