## What this proposal does

Adds a fleet PROPOSAL establishing the **monopoly optimal markup and the Lerner index** — a microeconomics / market-power head. A monopolist faces LINEAR inverse demand p(q) = a − b·q with constant marginal cost c (0 ≤ c < a, b > 0) and maximises profit π(q) = (a − c)·q − b·q². Setting MR = MC (a − 2b·q = c) gives q* = (a − c)/(2b), p* = (a + c)/2, π* = (a − c)²/(4b), and the resulting Lerner index of market power satisfies the EXACT identity L = (p* − c)/p* = (a − c)/(a + c) = 1/|ε*|, with |ε*| = (a + c)/(a − c) the absolute price-elasticity at the optimum. **The insight:** the optimal markup is exactly the RECIPROCAL of the elasticity the firm faces, and market power is a purely relative quantity — the Lerner index is unit-free (invariant to any currency rescale) and pinned entirely by the ratio (a−c)/(a+c). Headline instance a=1, b=1, c=1/4 → p*=5/8, q*=3/8, π*=9/64, L=3/5, 1/|ε*|=3/5. **Distinctness:** monopoly / Lerner index / optimal-markup / reciprocal-elasticity is a market-power head distinct from the coordination/consensus (P253 BFT sortition), Markov/queueing (Parrondo, Jackson, Erlang-C, PageRank), and game-combinatorics heads; the naive revenue-max foil (price at a/2) is pre-registered as G4 and rejected, which is what makes the profit-max-with-cost identity the point.
